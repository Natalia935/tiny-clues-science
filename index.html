What we do

Science workshops designed to feel exciting, premium, and real.

Tiny Clues Science creates immersive children’s science experiences where kids become scientists for the day. Each workshop combines real scientific ideas, guided experiments, colorful materials, and safe hands-on learning in a fun party environment.

Real Science Projects

Every activity is connected to real science concepts like chemistry, temperature, materials, reactions, space, or color science.

Real Scientist Set-up

Kids step into the role of scientists with a workshop setup designed to feel special, engaging, and photo-ready with lab coats and safety equipment.

Safe Guided Learning

The host leads the presentation, explains the experiment, and guides children through the activity step by step.

Birthday Parties, Workshops, Events

Science Party Packages

Every workshop includes a host-led science presentation, an age-appropriate explanation, and a hands-on experiment station where children perform the activities with guided help.

All parties last approximately 45 minutes to 1 hour.
