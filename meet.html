Meet Natalia

Hi! My name is Natalia, and I am the creator of Tiny Clues Science.

I am a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Illinois Chicago and I work on cryogenic research for the NASA-funded Terahertz Intensity Mapper mission.

TIM is a very cool telescope designed to be launched on a helium balloon about the size of a football stadium. Through my work and through Tiny Clues Science, my goal is to share knowledge, gain knowledge, and make every experiment feel exciting, meaningful, and fun.
